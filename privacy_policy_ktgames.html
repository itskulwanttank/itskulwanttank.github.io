2. Data we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Device and advertising identifiers & ad-related data: when you view or interact with ads in the App (served through Google AdMob and InMobi), advertising partners may automatically collect certain data such as device and app identifiers (including Advertising ID / AAID), Firebase Installation ID, IP address, app activity, and general location (e.g., city-level derived from IP or device signals, not precise GPS location) to serve, measure, and improve ads, analytics, security, and app functionality.

We do not collect payment card data or precise (GPS-level) location data. We do not sell user data.

3. How we use your data

We use data for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication via Firebase Phone Authentication.
  • Displaying and managing your profile (username, avatar, bio).
  • Enabling the public global chatroom and mention functionality.
  • Sending push notifications for chat mentions and essential system messages.
  • Moderation and enforcement by human moderators.
  • Improving App stability and performance using analytics and crash reports.
  • Running in-app giveaways and virtual in-game mechanics (coins, trophies, XP).
  • Displaying in-app advertisements via Google AdMob and measuring ad performance. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your consent and device settings.
