1. Introduction

KT Games ("we", "us") provides the mobile application KT Games — Play Win Repeat (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, with whom we share it, how long we keep it, and how you can exercise your privacy rights.

By using the App you consent to the collection and processing described here. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Data we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • User-provided: phone number (collected for Firebase Phone Authentication), display name / username, profile picture/avatar, short bio, and any files or images you upload to your profile.
  • Content you create: chat messages you post in the global chatroom (public to authenticated users).
  • Diagnostics & analytics: anonymous analytics and crash reports via Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (usage patterns, device model, OS version, crash logs).

We do not collect precise location data, advertising identifiers (AAID/IDFA), or payment card data. We do not sell user data.

3. How we use your data

We use data for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication via Firebase Phone Authentication.
  • Displaying and managing your profile (username, avatar, bio).
  • Enabling the public global chatroom and mention functionality.
  • Sending push notifications for chat mentions and essential system messages.
  • Moderation and enforcement by human moderators.
  • Improving App stability and performance using analytics and crash reports.
  • Running in-app giveaways and virtual in-game mechanics (coins, trophies, XP).

5. Public chat & visibility

The global chatroom is public to all authenticated users. Anything you post there (including your display name and avatar) will be visible to other users. Mentions may trigger push notifications to the mentioned user.

6. Giveaways & virtual currency

Giveaways are run in-app and winners are selected at random unless otherwise stated. All prizes are in-app virtual currency or items only — there are no real-money payouts. Virtual coins cannot be purchased, cashed out, or converted to real-world currency.

7. Age restriction

You must be 18 years or older to use this App. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we learn we have collected data from a minor in violation of this policy, we will promptly delete it.

8. Retention

Retention practices:

  • Chat messages: generally retained for approximately one week, after which they are deleted. Moderation or technical backups may cause short deviations.
  • User account/profile data: stored in Firebase Realtime Database and retained until you request deletion or the account is terminated by us per the Terms.

We will respond to verified requests submitted to the support email above.

10. Security

We use Firebase services and require authentication for account access. Moderators have elevated privileges to manage chat content. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data (encryption in transit, access controls). However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

11. Moderation & prohibited content

Human moderators review reported content and can delete posts, restrict accounts, or ban users for violations including cheating, harassment, exploitation, illegal activities, spam, or bots. Users can report messages in chat by tagging the user or using the report functionality. Moderation is performed periodically (approximately hourly).
